Preshow

Foreign soldiers from the United States of America have arrived with submarines, airplanes, and guns. We’ll see those soldiers in action, in film footage shot here in Iceland. You don’t want to miss that.

We’ll also see the American action star Roy Rogers performing with his horse Trigger straight from the wild west.

So come drop by at Sólvallagata 49 to see all the best that America has brought us here in the North.

Feature

Friðrik Þór Friðrikssons 1994 classic Bíódagar features a boy growing up in Reykjavík in the 50’s. A transaction period in Reykjavík culture is happening when american pop-culture enchants the local youth with movies, Rock and Roll, and all those exciting new things.
